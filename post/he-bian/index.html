河边

2025-05-04 1 min read

凉爽的风灌满我的宽大裤腿
在路上甩甩手臂 身后有小电动车飞过

双臂怎么画圈也无法驶离地面
噢，河边
麻雀走在我前面

Riverwalk

Cool wind pours into my wide pants legs,
my arms swing loosely as I walk.
Scooters zip past like a thought.

No matter how I spin,
the ground pulls me in.

Sparrows walk ahead of me
on the railing, then take flight—
certain of the way.
